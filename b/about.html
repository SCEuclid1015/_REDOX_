What is Humanoid ?

A humanoid robot is a robot designed to resemble the human body, equipped with a head, torso, arms, and legs.

These robots can mimic human actions and behaviors, making them capable of performing various tasks that require human-like interaction.

They utilize advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence, sensors, and actuators to navigate their environments, communicate with people, and execute complex movements.

Humanoid robots are increasingly used in fields like healthcare, education, and entertainment, highlighting their versatility and potential to enhance human experiences.
